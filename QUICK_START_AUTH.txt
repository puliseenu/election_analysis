TROUBLESHOOTING
================

Cannot login?
  - Check username spelling
  - Check CAPS LOCK
  - Ensure account is approved (not pending)
  - Reset admin: delete auth_database.db

Port 8050 in use?
  - taskkill /F /IM python.exe
  - Wait 2 seconds
  - Restart dashboard

Database locked?
  - Close other Python processes
  - Restart dashboard

No PENDING USERS showing?
  - Have someone register first
  - Click REGISTER button
  - Enter details
  - They appear in pending
